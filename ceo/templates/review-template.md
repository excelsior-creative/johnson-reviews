## Dek / summary

One or two sentences that tell readers who this review is for and the honest bottom line. No hype.

## Draft body

### Intro: why we went

Set the scene in Brandon's voice. Include the city/area early. Explain why this place was on the list and who was there.

### The experience

Use concrete moments from the intake:

- What we ordered / did
- Service moments
- What surprised us
- What fell short
- Any kid/family logistics that happened naturally

### Food / rooms / attraction details

For restaurants: dishes, texture, portions, price/value, what to reorder.

For hotels: room, sleep quality, amenities, fees, location tradeoffs.

For attractions: lines, age fit, duration, tickets, pacing, what to do differently.

### Family notes

Practical notes for parents and family travelers:

- Parking / arrival
- Wait/reservations
- Kid menu, stroller, bathrooms, noise
- Accessibility or mobility concerns if observed
- Best time to go

### What we'd do next time

Make the review useful. Examples: reserve earlier, skip a dish, request patio, park in a different lot, bring snacks, go on weekday.

### Verdict

Plainspoken recommendation:

- Who should go
- Who might skip it
- Whether Brandon would return

Avoid "must-visit" unless Brandon explicitly said it.
