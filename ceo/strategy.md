# Strategy

## What Johnson Reviews is

A personal brand built around **Brandon Johnson's actual
experiences as a traveling dad and restaurant-goer**. Reviews,
travel notes, and recommendations written in Brandon's own voice.
The authority we're building is: *this is a real family, eating
and traveling in real places, and Brandon's take is honest and
useful.*

## What Johnson Reviews is not

- Not a Michelin-guide / fine-dining-critic site. The reference
  design's visual language leans luxurious, but the content is
  family-first: Disneyland, Orange County restaurants, road
  trips with kids, Vegas with family. Don't let the aesthetic
  drag the voice into sommelier cosplay.
- Not an aggregator. Every review is a Johnson-family visit.
- Not a generic travel blog full of AI-adjacent "10 best" SEO
  listicles. Specificity and honesty are the moat.
- Not a hype machine. "MUST VISIT!!!" energy is banned. See
  voice.md.

## Audience

Primary: **other traveling families, especially in Southern
California and parents planning family vacations** (Disneyland,
San Diego, Vegas, coastal road trips, Puerto Vallarta). They
want: will my kids be happy here, is the parking a nightmare,
is there a kid menu, is it stroller-friendly, worth the drive?

Secondary: **locals looking for a trusted neighborhood voice on
restaurants** across Orange County, Hollywood/LA, San Diego.

Tertiary: general travel-curious readers who landed via Google.

## Positioning

*The traveling-dad review you'd trust over a stranger's Yelp.*
Written, not AI-slopped. Real kids. Real parking frustrations.
Real "the host walked out with a plate of something we didn't
order" details.

## Bets

1. **Voice + specificity wins.** Reviews that name the host, the
   exact dish, the stroller situation, will outperform generic
   listicles in both Google and in reader retention.
2. **SoCal family-travel is the beachhead.** Deep authority in
   a narrow geo first, expand outward.
3. **Schema-heavy, reviewer-focused SEO** (Review, Restaurant,
   LocalBusiness, AggregateRating) is cheaper than backlink
   wars and compounds with content volume.
4. **A conversational intake workflow** makes Brandon's time
   the last remaining bottleneck — once that's smooth, content
   velocity is only limited by trips, not by writing time.

## What we're explicitly deferring

- Newsletter (no list yet)
- Monetization (affiliates, ads, sponsorships) — wait until
  authority is established and traffic is material
- YouTube / TikTok / Instagram Reels production pipeline
- Booking integrations beyond basic affiliate links
