# OKRs — Q2 2026 (Apr–Jun)

First quarter with the CEO agent running. Goals are intentionally
modest; we're establishing the foundation before we push volume.

## O1. The redesign is live.

- **KR1.** Redesign per `reference/design/` shipped to production on
  johnsonreviews.com, covering: homepage, blog index, blog post / review
  detail, reviews index, about, contact. Target: end of April.
- **KR2.** Lighthouse performance ≥ 85 on the new templates (mobile),
  accessibility ≥ 95.
- **KR3.** No legacy color tokens (`#DB7D2D`) or Jost font references
  left in active frontend routes.

## O2. The review intake workflow is real.

- **KR1.** First review successfully drafted end-to-end via the
  workflow (Brandon mention → CEO interview → draft → Brandon
  redlines → published). Target: end of April.
- **KR2.** At least 5 more reviews land via the workflow by end of
  May.
- **KR3.** Median time from "Brandon mentions" → "review live" is
  under 72 hours (stretch: 48) by end of June.

## O3. The library starts growing again.

- **KR1.** At least 15 new reviews published in the quarter, all on
  real Johnson-family visits, all in calibrated voice.
- **KR2.** At least 3 distinct locations covered (e.g., Orange County,
  Las Vegas, a travel destination).
- **KR3.** Category/location hub pages exist for every category and
  every city with ≥ 2 reviews.

## O4. SEO and authority groundwork.

- **KR1.** Restaurant / TouristAttraction / Review schema correctly
  rendered on every published review.
- **KR2.** Internal link graph: every published review links to ≥ 2
  related reviews; category/location hubs link to their members.
- **KR3.** Sitemap complete and submitted; robots.txt sane.
- **KR4.** Baseline metrics captured in `metrics.md` by end of April:
  current traffic, current DA/DR, current indexed pages, current top 20
  queries.

## Explicitly deferred this quarter

- Monetization (ads, affiliates, sponsorships). No enrollment, no
  outreach, no pitches. Earn authority first.
- Email newsletter launch. Foundation only (capture form + double
  opt-in). Sending later.
- Instagram / Twitter / social. Drafts go to `content-drafts/` only;
  Brandon posts.
