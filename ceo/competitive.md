## What to watch, specifically

- **How do they write the opening paragraph of a review?** The best
  sites ground you in a specific moment immediately. Bad sites
  front-load "Nestled in the heart of…"
- **How do they handle photos?** Restaurant review sites with strong
  photography and minimal hype do disproportionately well.
- **How do they surface categories / locations?** Great hub pages
  are a differentiator.
- **Where are their backlinks coming from?** Patterns matter more
  than lists.
