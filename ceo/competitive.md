# Competitive landscape

_v0 — first-pass list to benchmark against. Brandon: who else is on
your list?_

## Family travel blogs (national)

- **The Points Guy / Family** — points-and-miles slant, big team,
  hard to outrank on award-flight stuff. Not a direct competitor.
- **Travel Babbo** — solo-author family travel, longer-form. Closer
  to Brandon's voice than most.
- **Trekaroo** — community-driven, declining.
- **Y Travel Blog** — Aussie family. Different geography mostly.
- **Pint Size Pilot** — family travel, Disney-heavy.

## Disney-specific

- **The Disney Food Blog (DFB)** — owns Disney food queries.
- **MouseHacking / Touring Plans / WDW Magic** — Disney-trip-planning
  authorities.
- **Mickey Visit** — Disneyland-leaning, OC-adjacent. Direct overlap
  on Brandon's Disneyland reviews.

## OC / SoCal local

- **OC Mom Collective** — family-focused OC. Direct overlap.
- **Mom on the Side** — OC family lifestyle.
- **OC Register food section** — local press, not a blog.
- **EaterLA / EaterSD** — restaurant-focused, professional.

## Restaurant review sites

- **Yelp** — incumbent, different format (short reviews + ratings).
- **Google Maps reviews** — Brandon already wins here. Site goal is
  to move people from "I read his Google review" → "I read his blog
  post."
- **The Infatuation** — professional, urban-focused.

## Where Johnson Reviews can win

- The intersection of **restaurant reviews + family travel + OC
  local + a recognizable personal name**. None of the above own all
  four at once.
- **Long-form, honest, voice-driven reviews of mid-tier places** —
  the Trader Vic's of "the Cha Cha's Latin Kitchen in Irvine"
  question. Eater won't write that. Brandon will.
- **Brandon's existing Local Guide footprint** is a moat: 500+
  reviews and 132M photo views is not a thing other personal-brand
  bloggers have.
