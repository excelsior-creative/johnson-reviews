### SEO infrastructure (the structured-data.ts overhaul)

- **`apps/app/src/lib/structured-data.ts`** rewritten:
  - `generateAuthorSchema()` — new. Person schema for Brandon, with
    `knowsAbout` knowledge graph. Single `@id` referenced from Article,
    Review, and Organization (`founder`).
  - `generateOrganizationSchema()` — now references the Author as
    `founder`.
  - `generateArticleSchema()` — now references Author by `@id` (no
    longer says the author is "Organization: Template Site"), uses
    excerpt as description, includes Category+Tag-derived `keywords`,
    handles missing `publishedDate` gracefully.
  - `generateReviewSchema(post, reviewMeta?)` — new. Maps the post to
    `Restaurant | Hotel | TouristAttraction | LocalBusiness` based on
    Categories+Tags string-match (with safe `LocalBusiness` fallback).
    **Does not fabricate ratings** — if `reviewMeta.rating` isn't
    provided, omits the `reviewRating` field entirely, since Google
    penalizes invented ratings.
  - `generateBreadcrumbSchema(post)` — new. Home → Reviews → Category
    → Title.
  - `generateGlobalSchema()` — now combines Org + WebSite + Author so
    every page's footer ships an authoritative author identity.
  - `combineSchemas()` — null-safe.
  - `absoluteImage()` helper for full-URL images in JSON-LD.
- **`apps/app/src/app/(frontend)/blog/[slug]/page.tsx`** — now renders
  the combined `Article + Review + Breadcrumb` JSON-LD inside each
  review page. Was completely missing before.
