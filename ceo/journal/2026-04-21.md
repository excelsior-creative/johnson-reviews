### 3. Killed a fabrication тАФ the Verdict block

The reviews/[slug] page had a "**The Verdict**" section that
rendered four rating axes тАФ **Ambience / Service / Value / Overall**
тАФ derived from the single overall rating by a formula:

```
Ambience = Math.max(60, rating*20 - 5)
Service  = Math.min(100, rating*20 + 2)
Value    = Math.max(60, rating*20 - 3)
```

**Brandon never rated any of those axes.** The site was printing
invented scores and presenting them as his. This is exactly the
fabrication the CEO mandate calls out ("No 'we loved the tiramisu'
unless Brandon told you they had the tiramisu"). It's gone.
Replaced with a simple honest rating summary + a line noting the
review was originally posted on Google.

Logged as **ADR 0002 тАФ No fabricated rating axes** so we don't
reintroduce this pattern.
